Our Product Range

Electronic Components Built to Spec

From standard catalogue parts to fully custom-engineered solutions — we manufacture components that meet the strictest industry tolerances.

PCBs & Assemblies

Single-layer to 32-layer PCBs, rigid, flex, and rigid-flex. Full SMT and through-hole assembly with AOI and X-ray inspection.

  • Min. trace width: 2 mil
  • Layer count: 1–32
  • Material: FR4, Rogers, Aluminium

Connectors & Cables

Board-to-board, wire-to-board, circular, RF, and high-speed connectors. Custom cable assemblies for harsh environments.

  • Pitch: 0.4 mm – 5.08 mm
  • Rated current: up to 100A
  • IP67 / MIL-SPEC options

Sensors & Modules

Temperature, pressure, proximity, motion, and environmental sensors. Wireless modules for IoT, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and LoRa.

  • Operating temp: −55°C to +155°C
  • Accuracy: ±0.1%
  • UART, SPI, I²C, CAN interfaces

Power Components

Capacitors, inductors, transformers, DC-DC converters, and power modules. Designed for high-reliability power delivery.

  • Voltage range: 3.3V – 1,500V
  • Efficiency: up to 98%
  • AEC-Q200 qualified options

Passive Components

Precision resistors, capacitors, inductors, crystals, and filters. Available in standard and anti-sulphuration grades.

  • Tolerance: ±0.01% – ±5%
  • Package: 0201 to through-hole
  • AEC-Q200 / IATF 16949

Careers at ElectroParts Co.

We invest in every engineer, technician, and operator on our team — and we take pride in the consistency that has kept our clients coming back since 1998. Below is an excerpt from our internal Employee Handbook and Brand & Communications Guidelines, the standards every new team member learns from day one.

📘

Employee Handbook (excerpt)

Essential standards reviewed in every new team member's first week, ensuring consistent quality and safety across every product line.

  • Quality & safety protocols: ESD-safe handling procedures, required PPE, and IPC-A-610 workmanship standards on the production floor.
  • Attendance & shift scheduling: documented shift-handover checklists and advance-notice requirements for time-off requests.
  • Role responsibilities: written SOPs for inspection, testing, and packaging stations across each product line.
  • Continuous improvement: every team member can submit process-improvement ideas through our kaizen program.
  • Career progression: a structured path from technician to line lead to process engineer, with skills-based certification tracks.
🎨

Brand & Communications Guidelines (excerpt)

A consistent voice and visual identity across every touchpoint — from technical documentation to client communications.

  • Logo & visual identity: approved usage of the ElectroParts mark, color palette, and typography across documentation and packaging.
  • Technical documentation: templates and formatting requirements for datasheets, quality certificates, and quotations.
  • Client communication: response-time targets and standard scripts for RFQ acknowledgements and order-status updates.
  • Trade show & marketing materials: approved templates for brochures, presentations, and exhibition signage.
  • Confidentiality & NDA protocols: guidelines for handling client drawings, BOMs, and proprietary specifications.
